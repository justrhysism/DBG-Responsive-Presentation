Also take note of the grid layout. Grid layouts are important for maintaining consistency and structure in a web design. They're not 100% essential, however they do make designs look nicer, and are much easier to code.

Boxes can easily be broken into sections, based on the grid. This grid is a 12-grid system.

This row is in thirds.

However it's important to note, that if you want boxes to appear like this, with gaps between each item, the technology doesn't exist to make them all the same height.

The spans don't all have to have the same width within a row. You can chop and change them around as much as you like!
